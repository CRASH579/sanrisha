About Sanrisha Greentech Private Limited

Sanrisha Greentech Private Limited (Sanrisha) has been operational since 2015 and actively collaborates with its sister organization Stepping Stone Foundation Trust. Together, they have worked on skill development under various government schemes such as:

  • Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojna (PMKVY)
  • Deen Dayal Upadhyay Gramin Kaushal Yojana (DDUGKY)
  • Uttar Pradesh Skill Development Mission (UPSDM)
  • National Urban Livelihood Mission (NULM)
  • Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives

Sanrisha focuses on mobilizing unemployed youth, especially the underprivileged, and training and certifying them in core sectoral skills as well as soft skills like Communication, IT, and Personality Development. The goal is to prepare them for job placements or self-employment.

Impact & Sectors

Sanrisha has empowered over 5,000 unemployed youth, especially from rural areas, and placed many in reputed companies across sectors like:

  • Retail
  • Telecom
  • Apparel
  • Construction
  • Logistics
  • IT

Operational primarily in Delhi NCR, Sanrisha aims to be the bridge between unemployed talent and companies in need of manpower, offering skilled, semi-skilled, and unskilled human resource solutions.
